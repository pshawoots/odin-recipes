Scrambled Eggs - Eggs Bhurji

Eggs bhurji are a type of scrambled eggs that have vegetables added to them.

This will be a basic recipe, you can add other vegetables such as green peppers or mushrooms if you want.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/2 a Red Onion
  • 1/2 a Tomato
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetable Oil

Instructions

  1. Dice your onion and tomato.
  2. Break your eggs and beat.
  3. Heat a pan with with some vegetable oil to medium-high.
  4. Add and Saute your diced onions for 2-3 minutes until it starts to brown.
  5. Add your tomatoes and keep mixing until diced tomatoes become slightly cooked.
  6. Add your eggs and salt, then stir continuously as it cooks.
  7. Cook to your preferred level of doneness.
